# Privacy Policy for Swipe Sprint

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Swipe Sprint ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## 1. Data We Collect

We may collect:

- Usage and gameplay data (for example: sessions, events, scores, ad interactions)
- Device and app information (for example: device type, OS version, app version)
- Advertising identifiers and related ad data (subject to your consent and platform settings)
- Crash and diagnostic data

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data.

## 2. How We Use Data

We use data to:

- Operate and improve the game
- Measure performance and fix bugs
- Analyze gameplay and retention
- Deliver and measure ads
- Prevent abuse and protect service integrity

## 3. Advertising and Analytics

Swipe Sprint uses third-party services, including:

- Google AdMob (ads)
- Google Firebase Analytics (analytics)

These providers may process data according to their own privacy terms.

## 4. Legal Basis and Consent (EEA/UK)

Where required by law, we request consent before processing data for personalized advertising.  
You can manage consent choices through in-app privacy options when available.

## 5. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS, we may request permission to track activity across apps/websites via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency prompt.  
You can change this choice anytime in iOS Settings.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, or as required by law.

## 7. Children

Swipe Sprint is not directed to children under the minimum age required by applicable law.  
If you believe a child has provided data improperly, contact us and we will review.

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, delete, or restrict processing of your data.
